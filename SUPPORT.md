# Mad Eggs Support

Thank you for playing **Mad Eggs**! 🥚🎮

If you encounter any issues, have questions, or need assistance, please use one of the options below:

## Contact Support
You can reach our support team directly at:  
**Email:** [support@bigbeemedia.co.uk](mailto:support@bigbeemedia.co.uk)  

Please include the following information when contacting support:  
- Your device model and iOS version  
- The version of **Mad Eggs** you are using  
- A brief description of the issue or question  

## Reporting Bugs
If you find a bug, please include:  
- Steps to reproduce the issue  
- Screenshots or screen recordings (if possible)  
- Any error messages you received  

## Suggestions and Feedback
We love feedback! Let us know what features or improvements you’d like to see in future updates.  

## Community
You can also follow and connect with us on social media:  
- [Big Bee Media Website](https://www.bigbeemedia.co.uk)  
- [Email](mailto:support@bigbeemedia.co.uk)  

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.
